# Chronos Engine Demo Storyboard

Target duration: 105 seconds.

| Time | Scene | Purpose |
|---:|---|---|
| 0-5s | Title card | Introduce Chronos Engine as AI decision intelligence from work history. |
| 5-13s | Landing page | Establish the product and the user's strategic decision goal. |
| 13-23s | Connect Data | Show connector-first ingestion: GitHub, Slack, Notion, upload, and demo-safe data. |
| 23-28s | Ingestion running | Show the app building a memory graph and evidence base. |
| 28-40s | Define Decision | Fill a career/portfolio positioning decision with three options. |
| 40-45s | Simulation running | Show evidence-backed futures being generated; waiting is accelerated. |
| 45-63s | Timeline branches | Show probabilities, expected regret, milestones, and confidence breakdowns. |
| 63-76s | Memory Graph | Show focused graph evidence and source labels. |
| 76-94s | Future Self answer | Ask for next priorities and show a grounded cited response. |
| 94-100s | Final card | Summarize value, stack, and repository. |

Primary message: Chronos turns fragmented work history into inspectable decision simulations with evidence, timelines, and grounded follow-up advice.
